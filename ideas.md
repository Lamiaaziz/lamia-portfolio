## Design System Extracted from PDF

- Feel: Clean, professional, calm, intelligent

<text>
**Approach A — "Soft Blueprint" (Chosen)**
Design Movement: Editorial Minimalism meets Scientific Illustration
Core Principles: Generous whitespace as breathing room; typographic hierarchy as navigation; subtle depth through layering; restraint over decoration
Color Philosophy: The portfolio's existing steel-blue palette is preserved and elevated — deep navy (#1C3D5A) as the anchor, warm off-white (#F7F9FC) as the canvas, with a single gold accent (#C9A84C) for call-to-action moments. The palette communicates intelligence and calm authority.
Layout Paradigm: Asymmetric editorial grid — hero section uses a large left-weighted typographic lockup with the name vertically stacked, case study cards use alternating left/right image-text layouts, no centered hero clichés.
Signature Elements: (1) Large faded circle motifs as section dividers (matching the PDF's decorative language); (2) Numbered section labels in light grey (01, 02, 03) as visual anchors; (3) Thin horizontal rules as breathing lines between sections.
Interaction Philosophy: Hover states reveal depth — cards lift with a subtle shadow; navigation links underline from left; project thumbnails scale gently on hover.
Animation: Fade-up entrance animations on scroll (framer-motion); staggered card reveals; no bouncy or playful motion — everything is deliberate and calm.
Typography System: Headings in "DM Serif Display" (editorial, distinctive, not Inter); body in "DM Sans" (clean, modern, readable); monospace accents for labels and tags.

## Selected Approach: **A — "Soft Blueprint"**
Chosen because it directly extends and elevates Lamia's existing PDF design language — the steel-blue palette, circle motifs, numbered sections, and calm editorial feel are all preserved and made interactive.
